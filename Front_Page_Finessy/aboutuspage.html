Students are so tired of working alone in this tedious process of fulfillment of their study abroad dream.
We have built a platform to help them out. Abroad Manager is the end to end solution to all their problems.

Our mission is to help and encourage students to experience a wonderful journey to a foreign university they have always wanted to study at.

We want students from around the world to leave their comfort zone and step forward to a place that is unknown. The visit to this unknown place is an opportunity to GROW.

We have brought a platform that provides a personal space for all the discussions related to almost every degree, every program and every university. This personal space helps students to easily dive into specific discussions of their interests.

Our platform is capable of solving problems related to:
  Profile Evaluation
  Career Counseling
  University Shortlisting
  Application Process
  Total Expenses Estimation
  Financial Planning
  Visa Process
  House Bookings

Our team at AbroadManager, work very hard to continuously improve the platform. We have the vision to become the best platform to help students for all study abroad problems around the world.
